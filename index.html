Snowflake Catcher

Left click to draw red stuff that catches the snowflakes. Right click to erase the red stuff. Press space bar to redraw the screen. The program uses mode 1 by default. Press "1" to choose mode 1 and "2" to choose mode two. Mode 1 uses the get() function. Mode 2 uses the more efficient pixels[] array.
